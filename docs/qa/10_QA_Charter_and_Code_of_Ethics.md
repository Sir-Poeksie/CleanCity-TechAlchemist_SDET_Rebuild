# CleanCity Waste Pickup Scheduler – QA Charter and Code of Ethics

**Purpose:** Define the principles, responsibilities, and ethical standards guiding Quality Assurance activities for the CleanCity Waste Pickup Scheduler platform.

**Scope:** Applies to all QA activities including functional, automation, performance, security, and accessibility testing across Web, API, and Mobile modules.

**Prepared by:** Mpumelelo Theophilas Nxazonke
**Date:** 2025-11-06
**Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. QA Charter

The QA team is committed to ensuring the **highest quality and reliability** of the CleanCity Waste Pickup Scheduler platform by adhering to the following principles:

1. **Integrity:** Report test results truthfully and objectively, without omission or alteration.
2. **Transparency:** Document and communicate testing scope, methods, coverage, and defects clearly to all stakeholders.
3. **Accountability:** QA is responsible for ensuring that all defects are tracked, validated, and addressed according to project priorities.
4. **Traceability:** All tests, test cases, and defects are traceable to functional and non-functional requirements (RTM maintained).
5. **Continuous Improvement:** QA practices are continuously reviewed and enhanced based on project learnings, retrospectives, and industry best practices.

---

## 2. QA Roles & Responsibilities

| Role                               | Responsibilities                                                                              |
| ---------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Fullstack SDET                     | Implement and maintain automated and manual test suites, ensure traceability, report defects. |
| QA Lead / Test Manager             | Approve QA charter, strategy, and plan; oversee QA compliance and execution.                  |
| Developers                         | Support test environment setup, resolve defects, maintain code quality.                       |
| Product Owner / BA                 | Provide clear requirements and acceptance criteria; clarify scope and priorities.             |
| Security/Accessibility Specialists | Validate compliance with OWASP, GDPR, and WCAG standards.                                     |

---

## 3. QA Code of Ethics

QA activities must comply with the following ethical standards:

1. **Confidentiality:** Protect sensitive user and business data encountered during testing.
2. **Impartiality:** Conduct testing objectively; avoid personal bias when assessing software quality.
3. **Professionalism:** Uphold industry best practices (ISTQB, ISO, OWASP, WCAG) in all QA tasks.
4. **Respect:** Collaborate respectfully with developers, stakeholders, and team members.
5. **Compliance:** Ensure adherence to legal, regulatory, and organizational standards.

---

## 4. Enforcement & Review

* QA charter adherence is monitored by QA Lead/Test Manager.
* Violations or ethical concerns are reported and escalated according to company policy.
* Charter reviewed quarterly or after major project milestones to maintain alignment with industry best practices.
